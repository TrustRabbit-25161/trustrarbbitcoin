About Us

A Legacy of Institutional Investment

Headquartered in the United States, we are a financial investment company built on the foundation of public market discipline and long-term capital stewardship.

Trust Rabbit Capital is a U.S.-based financial investment group with roots in publicly listed capital markets. For over a decade, our team has deployed capital across high-growth internet platforms, fintech infrastructure, and digital asset ecosystems.

We have invested in and partnered with leading internet companies at every stage of growth — from early-stage innovation to global scale. Our network extends across the digital economy, including strategic collaborations with blockchain projects, exchange platforms, and virtual asset companies worldwide.

Trust Rabbit (TRBT) represents our commitment to transparent, on-chain financial infrastructure on the TRON network — combining institutional-grade governance with the efficiency of decentralized technology.
